What is it?

Mental wellbeing, as stated by the Scottish Government is our internal positive view that we are coping well psychologically with the everyday stresses of life and can work productively and fruitfully. On the other hand, Mental illness is a health condition that affects emotions, thinking and behaviour, which substantially interferes with or limits our life.

How to achieve it

The National Health Institue (UK)has provided five ways to improve or achieve mental health and well-being. Some of the methods are actually related to the other kinds of well-being showing how interconnected they may all be.

  • Have good relationships or connections
  • Carry out regular physical activity
  • Learn new skills
  • Give to others and be kind
  • Be mindful

Fun Facts!

  • Laughter releases endorphins, reducing stress and improving mood.
  • Hugging someone releases oxytocin, the "bonding hormone," which can increase feelings of trust, security, and well-being
  • Interacting with animals can reduce feelings of loneliness, lower stress levels, and increase feelings of happiness and well-being.
