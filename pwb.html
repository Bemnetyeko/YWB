What is it?

According to the Australia National University, physical wellbeing is maintainting a healthy life in which we can do most of our daily activities without getting tired easily and feeling physically stressed. "It includes taking care of our bodies and recognizing that our daily habits and behaviours have a significant impact on our overall health, wellbeing and quality of life."

How to achieve it

There are different things we can do to maintain a healthy physical wellbeing. The following are some ways to achieve it as stated by BetterUp:

  • Eat Well
  • Exercise
  • Get enough sleep
  • Stay hydrated
  • Drink in moderation

Fun Facts!

  • A longer workout isn't necessarily a better workout!
  • Strength training continues to burn fat long after you finish your workout
  • Exercise can improve your focus, concentration and memory
