What is it?

According to BetterUp, emotional health is how you are able to manage your thoughts, feelings, and emotions when times are tough. Emotionally healthy people can control their thoughts, feelings, and actions and can "bounce back" the different challenges in life.

How to achieve it

These are some of the ways you can achieve emotional wellbeing according to National Institutes of Health (NHI- US)

  • Cultivate a positive mindset
  • Practice gratitude in your daily life
  • Get quality sleep
  • Strengthen your social connections
  • Be mindful

Fun Facts!

Here are some fun facts from6seconds:

  • Emotions are absorbed in the body in about six seconds
  • Emotions function to guide us to survive and thrive
  • Emotions are electrochemical signals that flow through us in an unending cycle
