What is it?

According to Northwestern University, spiritual wellbeing is the "expanding a sense of purpose and meaning in life, including one's morals and ethics." It also does not always involve religious beliefs.

How to achieve it

There are many ways to maintain spiritiual health. Banner Health states things to do to maintain your spiritual health even if you do not have a specific religion or even outside of your religion.

  • Connect with your faith community (church, temple...)
  • Meditate
  • Spend time in nature
  • Keep a journal
  • Practice yoga

Fun Facts!

  • According to a study conducted by the Mayo Clinic, people who are spiritual or religious often live longer than others.
  • Sagelifestates that Spirituality can deliver a sense of well-being, flooding the brain with immunity-boosting hormones.
  • The positive feelings associated with spirituality can lower stress levels and blood pressure and can have a significant impact on lifestyle choices.
